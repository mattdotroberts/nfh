How to join the private community

  1. First, download Geneva to your mobile device here. Downloading the app isn’t necessary, but I guarantee you’ll have a better time with the community if you do.

  2. Read the code of conduct. The New Fatherhood is an open, diverse community, I want it to be a place where you bring your best self, and connect with other like-minded dads without fear of harassment.

  3. OK now join the community here:

    Jump in

    Please don’t share this link! If you know someone who would benefit from this community but can't pay, please tell them to get in touch and I’ll gift them a free subscription.

  4. When you join, you'll get a message introducing you to the community. Jump into the Intros channel, and say hello. Ask some Questions, share your Parenting Tips or take a seat on the Therapy Couch and share how you’re feeling. Any share any Recommendations for music, TV, podcasts or films that you’re enjoying.

  5. Right now, I'm keeping it to these channels while we build the community up. But if there's something you'd like to see, let me know in the Suggestions channel.

Any questions? Let me know.

Upcoming community events

As part of the community, you’re also invited to the weekly “TNF Summit” which is what we’re calling a very low-key talk between dads, occurring every 2 weeks or so. I can’t bring myself to call it a Zoom / Videochat / Meeting, it feels too much like work. But “Summit” gives it the appropriate delusion of grandeur.

Most, but not all, events are at European / US-friendly times, with a few working for folks in NZ / Australia. The full events schedule of upcoming events can be seen here and you can easily sign up and it’ll all get added to your calendar (but only if you want it!). Certain in-real-life events will also show up on that link, as well as in the Geneva app.
