This week I realised that whilst I’ve spilt a lot of ink on work and careers—that you are not your job, the death of the commute, filling your purpose bucket, how to quit your job and where you might live if you did—I’ve never asked that age-old question:

What do you do, and how is it going?

So let’s use this space this weekend to talk about work. Do you work for yourself, or run a small business? If you work for someone else, are you happy there, or have the last few years got you thinking of a change? Are you back in the office now? Hybrid? Fully remote? What about the biggest stresses you’re facing at work? And some of the things you love about what you do?

Come on, come all. Pull up a chair. Let’s talk work.
